## 1. Orientation & Navigation
- **Sidebar**: Your primary navigation for **Dashboard**, **Engine**, **Pilot™**, **Ad Lab™**, **Analytics**, and **Settings**.

## 2. VoxMirror™: Brand DNA Extraction
Before you create, you must **Mirror**. This process extracts your brand's "Sacred Truth" to ensure every AI-generated asset feels authentic.

### How to use:
1. Go to the **Dashboard** or **Engine** tab.
2. Enter your **URL** or a **Topic/Bio**.
3. Click **Initialize Mirror**. 
4. **DNA Lock**: Once complete, your **Tone**, **Campaign Anchors**, and **Visual Palette** are "Locked" into the Engine.

## 3. Persona Sync & The Chameleon Engine
VoxVivid doesn't just "store" your brand; it **becomes** your brand. When your DNA is synced:
- **White-Labeling**: The app's theme (colors, accents, and surfaces) automatically shifts to your brand palette.
- **Brand Avatar**: Your logo replaces the default studio icon in the Sidebar.
- **Studio Identity**: Your page title updates to `VoxVivid | [Your Brand] Studio`.
- **Contrast Guard**: Our engine automatically adjusts text and button colors to ensure your brand's unique palette remains perfectly readable across all devices.

## 4. The Ad Lab™: Infinite Creative
The Ad Lab is where you compose high-fidelity creative variants across Image, Video, Music, and Voice.

### Core Workflows:
- **Magic Draft™**: Use the "Refresh Ideas" button to get AI-suggested prompts tailored to your brand.
- **Vibe & Vision**: 
  - **Vision Lock**: Upload a product image to anchor the AI's visual generation to your actual product. **Highly recommended for video**: Providing a starting image acts as a "seed frame," resulting in 99% better video consistency.
  - **VoxMusic (Lyria AI)**: Toggle "Music" to generate a unique sonic backdrop.
  - **VoxVoice (ElevenLabs)**: Toggle "Voice" to add a professional voiceover.
- **Storming**: Click **Storm More** to generate up to 12 variations of a single concept.
- **Carousel Mode**: Toggle "Carousel" to create a cohesive story across multiple 4:5 or 9:16 cards.

Pulse tracks trends and relevance scores in real-time.
- **Spike %**: Indicates how fast a trend is growing.
- **Relevance**: How well a trend aligns with your **Locked DNA**.
- **Action**: High-relevance spikes should be used as prompts in the **Ad Lab** for maximum impact.

## 7. The Share Suite: Approved & Published
Once your creative is ready, the **Share Suite** gives you three ways to move from the studio to the feed.

### Native Share (Direct Posting)
- **What it is**: Direct API integration with your social accounts.
- **Platforms**: Official support for **Instagram Reels**, **TikTok**, and **Discord**. 
- **How to use**: Connect your accounts in **Settings** and click `Direct Share` on any finalized variant.

### Cringe Check™ (Feedback Loop)
- **What it is**: A unique, passwordless review link for external collaborators.
- **The Reactions**: Reviewers can react with **Slay**, **Okay**, **Mid**, or **Cringe** and leave specific notes.
- **Notifications**: You'll receive a **Web Push notification** and an in-app alert the moment feedback is submitted.

### One-Time Publish Codes
- **What it is**: A secure 8-character code that allows a guest to publish a specific ad *without* needing access to your account.
- **Duration**: Codes are valid for **24 hours** and limited to 5 per day for security.

## 8. Deep Edit: Refining Your Vision
The Ad Lab isn't just a "one-and-done" generator; it's a collaborative workspace. If a variant is close but not perfect, use **Deep Edit** to polish it.

### How to Refine (Changing the Prompt)
- **The Refinement Bar**: Select a variant and look for the "Refine this creative..." bar at the bottom.
- **Specific Instructions**: You can type conversational commands like *"Make the headline more punchy"* or *"Rewrite the primary text for a Gen-Z audience."*
- **The Result**: The AI treats your selected variant as a "base" and generates a new, refined version based on your feedback.

### Can I edit the text directly?
- **No Manual Typing**: To maintain the "Sacred Truth" of your brand and ensure high-conversion layouts, you cannot click and type over the headline.
- **Ask the AI**: Simply use the Refinement Bar. For example: *"Change the headline to: 'The Best Coffee in London'."*

### Can I change the image? (Vision Lock Update)
- **Swap at any time**: Use the **Vision** (Camera) button in the Vibe & Vision deck to upload a new product photo.
- **Precision Re-Generation**: Once a new image is uploaded, click the **Refine** arrow. The AI will re-compose the ad using your new visual anchor while keeping your refined prompt.

## 10. VoxGuardian™: Data Privacy & Security
Your data is your competitive advantage. We protect it with a "Fort Knox" philosophy.

### Is my information safe?
- **Bank-Grade Encryption**: We use **AES-256-GCM** encryption (the gold standard for global finance and military communications) to protect your Brand DNA.
- **Encrypted at Rest**: Your "Sacred Truth" is encrypted before it ever hits our database. To an outsider, your brand data looks like random, unbreakable code.
- **Data Isolation**: Your DNA is **never** used to train public AI models. It remains in a private, locked vault dedicated exclusively to your account.
- **Secure Sessions**: We enforce strict "Single Active Session" rules. If you log in elsewhere, your old session is instantly killed to prevent unauthorized access.

### What if VoxVivid is compromised?
- **Zero-Knowledge Architecture**: Even if an unauthorized party accessed our servers, they would find only "gibberish" (encrypted strings). Without your unique account context, your brand's secrets remain unreadable and safe.

## 11. FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Why does DNA extraction take 45-60 seconds?
**A:** We use a "Creative Cascade" of flagship AI models (Gemini Pro, GLM-5). These models perform deep visual and textual analysis in parallel to ensure 99% brand consistency.

### Q: I'm seeing a "Session version conflict" error. What happened?
**A:** VoxVivid enforces a **Single Active Session** for security. If you log in on a new device or tab, your previous session is invalidated to protect your Brand DNA. Simply refresh to continue in your newest session.

### Q: What's the difference between "Storming" and "Generating"?
**A:** **Generating** initializes a new creative concept. **Storming** uses your selected concept to create a diverse range of visual and copy variants based on the same prompt.

### Q: How do I export my ads to Meta or TikTok?
**A:** Select a variant in the **Ad Lab**, ensure it is **Locked**, and click **Finalize & Export**. You can then download the assets or use a **Cringe Link** to share the review session with your team.

### Q: Can I have multiple Brand Identities?
**A:** Currently, each account supports one **Primary Brand DNA**. Extracting a new site will overwrite your existing DNA. For multi-brand support, please contact our Alchemist support team.
