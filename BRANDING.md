Welcome to the VoxVivid Brand Kit. This directory acts as the source of truth for the **Sovereign Era (v5.2.0)** visual identity, centered on the **Neural Signature V** and the **Obsidian Sapphire** core.

## 🎨 Visual Identity (Sovereign Revision)

Our visual system is built on "Quiet Luxury"—a premium, high-fidelity aesthetic that emphasizes brand sovereignty and surgical precision.

| Element | Specification | HSL |
|---------|---------------|-----|
| **Core Accent** | Sapphire Blue (`#3B82F6`) | `hsl(217, 91%, 60%)` |
| **Primary Base** | Obsidian Sapphire (`#0A0C12`) | `hsl(222, 28%, 5%)` |
| **High-Fi Glow** | Emerald Pulse (`#10B981`) | `hsl(160, 84%, 39%)` |
| **Neural Glow** | Signature Magenta (`#FF00AA`) | `hsl(320, 100%, 50%)` |

### Typography
- **Headings**: Space Grotesk (700wt, 0.1em tracking) — Bold, geometric, and authoritative.
- **Body**: Inter (400wt) — Clean, legible, and modern.

### Iconography: The Neural Signature V
The **Neural Signature V** (Split-blade architecture) is the absolute visual anchor of the VoxVivid brand. It represents the intersection of neural intelligence and brand sovereignty.
