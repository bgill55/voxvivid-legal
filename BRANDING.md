# VoxVivid Branding: Sacred Pedigree 💎⚔️

Welcome to the VoxVivid Brand Kit. This directory acts as the source of truth for our visual identity, centered on the **Neural Signature V** and the **Obsidian Sapphire** core.

## 📂 Asset Structure

All high-fidelity assets are located in `/public/branding`:

- **`/icons`**: High-resolution "Neural Signature V" logos.
- **`/social`**: Cinematic social preview images (OG images) in Obsidian.
- **`/fonts`**: Space Grotesk (Headings) and Inter (Body).
